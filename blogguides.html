Our Story

Founded in Nov 2016, Park City UT’s Uncharted Supply Co’s mission is to provide high-quality, intuitive gear to help people navigate emergencies big and small.

Uncharted’s first product, The Seventy2 Survival System, reimagined the personal preparedness space and launched an industry.

From their appearance on Shark Tank, to placements in the Museum of Modern Art, to the product selected for use by government and private security agencies around the world, Uncharted has continued to design products that have saved lives around the world.

Today, Uncharted’s expanded product line is available in over 200 retail stores, and provides coverage for the adventurer as well as those looking to prepare for an ever-changing world.

Essential Tips for Emergency Preparedness

Emergencies can strike without warning, but with the right preparation, you can face any challenge confidently. Follow these essential tips to ensure your safety and the safety of your loved ones:

  • Build a Kit: Assemble a survival kit with food, water, first aid supplies, flashlights, batteries, and essential tools.
  • Create a Plan: Identify emergency contacts, establish meeting points, and make sure everyone in your household knows the plan.
  • Stay Informed: Sign up for local alerts, monitor weather conditions, and keep a battery-powered radio handy.
  • Practice Regularly: Conduct drills to ensure everyone knows what to do in case of fire, earthquake, or other emergencies.

Preparedness can make all the difference. Take steps today to protect what matters most.
